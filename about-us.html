A brief history

Malou Pet Haven was founded in 2008 by animal lover and activist Malou Rodriguez. Initially a small community effort to rescue stray animals in her hometown, it has since grown into a beacon of hope for abandoned and neglected pets. Over the years, the shelter has saved and rehomed thousands of animals, providing them with a second chance at life. The shelter was built on the principles of compassion, responsibility, and community engagement, driven by the belief that every animal deserves a loving home.

Today, Malou Pet Haven operates as a leading non-profit organization, supported by dedicated volunteers, generous donors, and a shared passion for animal welfare.

Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals in need while promoting responsible pet ownership and community involvement. We are committed to reducing animal homelessness and improving the welfare of pets through education, spay/neuter programs, and adoption services.

We envision a world where no pet is left without a home and every life is valued.

Malou Pet Haven Shelter is located at:
